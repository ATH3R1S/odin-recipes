Honey Glazed Chicken

Description:

A light meal that everyone will enjoy. Serve with steamed rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1.5 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; Set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly
    brown for about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet, continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer
    pink in the center and the sauce is thickened,about 5 minutes more
  5. Serve hot and enjoy.
